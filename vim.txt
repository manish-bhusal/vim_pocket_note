VIM:- VIM(Vi iMproved) is a text editor for Unix-based systems. It is a command-line based editor and is considered to be an advanced version of the original Vi editor. VIM is often used by programmers, system administrators and power users. It's customizable, cross-platform and open-source.

Most common keys:-
i-insert
O-insert on next line
a-insert after a space
Esc-Escape out of any mode
:q-Quit
:w-save without exiting
:q!-quit without saving
:wq-quit and save
r-replace
d-delete(2 times and that line will be deleted)
u-undo
x-remove one character
backspace-
/-forward search
?-backward search
